🚀 Quick Setup

Get started in minutes

Simple installation process compatible with Taskify v2.0.0 and above

1

Download Plugin

Get the plugin ZIP file from your CodeCanyon purchase and save it to your computer for installation.

2

Access Admin Panel

Login as Administrator and navigate to Settings > Plugins section in your Taskify dashboard.

3

Install & Activate

Upload the plugin ZIP file and activate it through the plugins management interface.

4

Clear Cache

Clear your Taskify cache to ensure optimal performance and proper plugin integration.

5

Access Assets Section

Find the new "Assets" section in your Taskify sidebar navigation menu.

6

Start Managing

Create categories, upload your assets, and begin assigning them to your team members!
